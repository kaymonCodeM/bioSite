The majority of Hannah’s hobbies revolve around art. Most of her works are done in watercolors and mixed media, but she also does Graphic Art and Fiber Art as well. Water coloring is an interesting type of painting style that allows the paint to be soluble in the water and flow and move more organically on the page. Mixed media art is a mixture of different paints, textures, inks, and materials on the same surface. Graphic Art, one of Hannah’s newest interests, usually involves a tablet and a pen to make fantastic sketches. Tufting, her most recent passion, is a special kind of skill that helps people to create rugs and imagery using a tufting gun (essentially a portable sewing machine), this is where she is beginning her skills in fiber arts.

If Hannah is not doing artwork at home, you will usually see her in a coffee shop. This is where she likes to chill and drink a smooth cup of coffee and sometimes do artwork as well. Hannah is usually working but when she is not, she likes to hangout with friends and family. A lot of her family lives outside of Lincoln so there is usually a lot of traveling involved which she loves because she gets to adventure out into different states.

Hannah is a relaxed person; all it takes for her to me rejuvenated is to take time to go out and enjoy nature. She loves taking nature walks and doing photography walks with her boyfriend whenever she gets the chance. Thrifting to find nice clothes in the local stores is also one of her specialties. She can almost find anything to wear within any shop she goes in. When Hannah was in high school, she used to be the baker in the cafeteria, to this day, she still enjoys baking and trying new recipes and sharing her treats with those she loves.
